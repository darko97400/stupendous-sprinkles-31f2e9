About the game

Command Bouba, Big Bob,
and a growing army across
Rustoria and beyond.

Jump into VR and command your miniature army from above like a tabletop general. Fight across compact battlefields, capture buildings, unleash vehicles, and explore the world of Rustoria with Bouba and Big Bob — with other planets planned too.

Unlock progression Unlock plenty of units and vehicles as you keep playing.
Track your war Know your Robots Scrapped score across the whole game or during the current match.
Free support Free updates and free DLC are planned to expand the experience over time.
Future multiplayer Multiplayer is in development for even more tabletop chaos.

The world of Project S

Bouba, Big Bob, Rustoria, and more places to conquer.

Project S mixes epic sci-fi presentation with a playful personality. The world is full of chunky robots, glowing eyes, heavy metal armor, and fun cinematic moments that give the game a memorable identity.

  • Readable silhouettes and strong blue-vs-red faction contrast
  • Rustoria is only the beginning — more planets can expand the universe
  • Perfect for trailers, social posts, merch, and key art
